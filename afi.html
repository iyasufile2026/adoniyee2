Day 5 — Your Strength
There's something I understand now… something I didn't fully see before. You're strong. Not in the loud way people talk about— but in the quiet way… the real way. The kind of strength it takes to carry something you haven't fully let go of. I see it. The way part of you still feels something… even after everything. The way you never got the closure you deserved. The way some emotions don't just disappear just because time has passed. And still… you keep going. That's not weakness. That's strength. I won't lie… At first, it confused me. Maybe even disturbed me. Not because of what you feel— but because I didn't know where I stood. But now I understand something. I don't love you just because you're with me. I love you because… you're the person I want to spend my life with. Not a perfect version of you. Not a version without a past. But you… exactly as you are. And I think… that's what makes this real.
