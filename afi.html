Day 3 & Day 4 — Who You Are
The things people don't notice right away

My Dearest,

One thing I've found myself doing, without even realizing it… is trying to understand you.

To read you.

Every little detail.
Every movement.
Every quiet reaction.

And somehow… every time I think I've figured you out—
you surprise me again.

Not in a loud way.
Not in a way that demands attention.

But in the way your personality quietly reveals itself…
and somehow says more than words ever could.

Even the way you speak—
you don't say too much,
yet it never feels like something is missing.

It's enough.
You are enough.

There's something else I noticed too.

Whenever I talk to someone,
there's always this small tension in me…

What if they don't understand me?
What if I say the wrong thing?

But with you…

that feeling disappears.

You understand me so easily,
so naturally,
like it was never something I had to worry about in the first place.

And that… is rare.

But the part that stays with me the most—

is your kindness.

The way you treat people.
The way you carry softness without weakness.
The way you care… even when no one is asking you to.

It's not something you try to show.
It's just who you are.

Even in small moments—
if I say something wrong,
you don't ignore it.

You either gently correct me…
or I can see it in your reaction—
like your heart simply refuses to accept anything unkind.

And honestly…

that part of you?

I really, really like it.

Because it tells me something important.

That your heart isn't just kind—
it's real.

And in a world where people pretend so much…

that makes you rare.

Quietly certain about you,

Always yours
